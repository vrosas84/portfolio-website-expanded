Upgrades, people. Upgrades.

“The secret of making progress is to get started.”

The next big update should be posting my python projects I did recently. Nothing earth shattering but it'll be nice to get that content moved over. I also should make a portfolio/projects page.

Calling it a night...maybe

I don't plan on resting on my rear after completing these next couple of upgrades. I want to keep up this progress. Learning more frontend and design will only make me more attractive to potential employers (Not that I have plans to leave my current employer)

Knowing more frontend can help with that too though. One of the platforms we use is built on a platform called Thingsboard. Once I get more comfortable with general web technologies, I'd like to learn more about Thingsboard.

Who knows, maybe once I'm proficient enough with web technologies, both frontend and backend, maybe I'll redesign that website to be more efficient and useful. I have my issues with it, but of course I won't get into that in a public space.
